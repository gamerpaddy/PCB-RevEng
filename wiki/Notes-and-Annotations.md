# Notes and Annotations

Sticky notes let you pin reminders to the board — "check this diode", "R12 value unclear", "power comes in here" — without drawing over the photo or getting in the way of your work.

## Adding a note

1. Click the **note** tool in the top bar (the 📝 sticky-note icon).
2. Click anywhere on the board to drop a note there.
3. Type the text of the note.

A small marker stays on the board where you dropped it. The full text does not sit on top of the board cluttering it — instead it appears in a little bubble only when you hover the marker, so notes never obstruct what is underneath.

## Reading and editing notes

- Hover a note marker to read its text.
- With the **Select** tool, click a marker to select the note; its text and colour show in the Inspector, where you can edit them.
- Drag a marker to move the note somewhere else.

## Colour

Each note has a colour you can change in the Inspector, so you can use colour to sort notes by kind — for example one colour for things to double-check and another for finished observations.

## Notes are saved with the project

Notes are part of your project. They are kept by the automatic browser save and are written into the project file when you click **Save**, so they travel with the board.
